Pixelmapping Workshop
January 2024

Illumination has the power to capture and induce moods, especially in audio-visual settings like on a dance floor. As part of my artistic vision to share knowledge, power and responsabilities I organized a pixelmapping workshop on my custom-built light fixtures with my technical set-up. The goal was to empower participants to have some basic understanding of pixelmapping, the basic workings of the lights, and subsequentially do their own live visuals.

Location: Berlin, Germany
Role: Trainer
Duration: 1 Day
