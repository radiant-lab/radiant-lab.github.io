about

Radiant lab is a Berlin based artist implementing custom light projects to create unique visual experiences. Complementary to the respective sound and space radiant lab's light hardware and live visuals emphasize diverse moods and induce atmospheres exceeding the possibilities of conventional light.

Target wavelenghts from 380 to 750 nanometers.

workshops

Illumination has the power to capture and produce moods, especially in audio-visual settings like on a dance floor. However, there are (structural) barriers to becoming a visual artist or light technician, such as financial constraints or knowledge hierarchies. An important part of my vision is to empower others to express themselves artistically in different ways, breaking barriers between the artists and the audience, and making art accesible. My participartory approach to light art encompasses giving workshops for skill sharing and providing the set-up and space for others to do live visuals during events. Workshops include (but are not limited to) Introduction to LED electronics, my custom-built light fixtures, and pixelmapping with different software. Everytime people step up from the dancefloor into the light booth I am amazed by their energy, passion and creativity.
